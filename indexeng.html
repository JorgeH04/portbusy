Presentación

The importance of electronic commerce as a business tool is growing exponentially. The option of publishing your products on the networks so that they can later contact you by whatsapp has a limit. The client does not have patience when he sees that he has to contact by whatsapp and wait for an answer, hence the importance of automating the purchase process. Relying more and more on the security offered by means such as Mercadopago or Paypal, there is a revolution in the sales techniques of SMEs and large companies. On a website you can contain detailed information not only about your business but about each product. Simplify the shopping experience, possibility of organizing your stock in a single place, sell at any time.
